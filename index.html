History Revision Resources for GCSE and A Level

GCSE A level Exemplar Revision Resources Recommended Websites

Please take advantage of some of the resources available on this website! I am a Head of History at a North Yorkshire school and have built these revision resources with the exam spec and core textbooks. In themselves, they are not designed to be engaging but instead should be used as the basis for collaborative revision or forming your own revision strategies. There are two types of revision resources available for GCSE, and currently one for A Level. Details on best practice to revise these is included on the relevant pages. The following topics are currently available on this website.

  • Edexcel GCSE Option 11: Medicine in Britain, c1250–present and The British sector of the Western Front, 1914–18: injuries, treatment and the trenches
  • Edexcel GCSE Option B1: Anglo-Saxon and Norman England, c1060–88
  • Edexcel GCSE Option P3: The American West, c1835–c1895
  • Edexcel GCSE Option 31: Weimar and Nazi Germany, 1918–39
  • AQA A Level Option 1C The Tudors: England, 1485–1603
